Brain Mind and Body- Where is the self?

“I think, therefore I am” – René Descartes. Once stated by one of the most influential philosophers in the history, Descartes, the only certain undoubted property is the doubting soul itself. Descartes concludes that the human soul is an immaterial entity, detached from the physical world. Thereby the wise philosopher stumbled upon a problem that remains unsolved - the problem of mind and body, the interactive relationship between the mind and the body or more specifically, the brain.
